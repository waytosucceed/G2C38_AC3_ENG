📌 Common Prepositions and How We Use Them:

  • In: This word tells us when something is inside something else.
    Example: "The cat is in the box." 🐱📦
    This means the cat is inside the box!
  • On: This word shows that something is resting on a surface.
    Example: "The book is on the table." 📖🪑
    Here, the book is sitting on top of the table.
  • Of: This word helps us show a connection between things.
    Example: "The cover of the book is red." 📕
    This means the red cover belongs to the book.
  • Under: This word tells us that something is below another thing.
    Example: "The dog is under the bed." 🐶🛏️
    This means the dog is beneath the bed.
  • Above: This word shows that something is higher than another thing.
    Example: "The bird is flying above the trees." 🐦🌳
    The bird is up in the sky, higher than the trees.
  • At: This word tells us the specific place where something happens.
    Example: "We are meeting at the park." 🌳🏞️
    This means we are gathering in the park.
  • For: This word helps show purpose or who something is meant to help.
    Example: "This gift is for my friend." 🎁😊
    It means the gift is intended to make my friend happy.
